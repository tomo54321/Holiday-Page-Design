About

Kilnwick Percy Resort is part of our Evermore Lodge Holidays Collection, the ultimate in über-cool luxury set in a 200-acre country estate. A perfect retreat for creating cherished memories with family and friends.


Radiating light, space and style, with all lodges offering hot tubs, the last word in indulgent relaxation, these fabulous lodges cannot fail to impress. The superb KP Estate completes the picture – the chic Glasshouse hosts fine cuisine whilst its terrace overlooking rolling Yorkshire countryside is perfect for a sundowner!


Picturesque walks in woodland, cycle routes and bustling market towns define this lovely area. Quaint Pocklington town is only a mile away, while exquisite York is just 15 miles further. East Yorkshire’s stunning beaches are easily accessible and there is a water sports centre just 4 miles away offering kayaking and pedaloes for the ones seeking a bit of adventure.


The KP Estate is a superb retreat in which to enjoy time with family, friends or just the two of you. Enjoy fine cuisine in the architect designed Glasshouse or relax and enjoy a drink on the terrace over looking the rolling Yorkshire countryside. Just a mile from the quaint town of Pocklington, less than 15 miles from the historic town of York and under an hour from the stunning East Yorkshire coastline make KP Lodges a perfect location for a short break or relaxing family holiday.
